My Ethics and values lie in authenticity. One of the most beautiful aspects of human nature is the uniqueness of human interaction. You can find people extremely similar, but you cannot find people exactly the same. The same can be said about the workspace; you come across a plethora of different individuals, and it should be expected that everyone has their own ways of approaching work as themselves, not trying to mimic the work of another. 



Another aspect of ethics is accountability. People lack the ability to be accountable for their actions. Failures are blamed on other factors rather than looking internally. Problems are created by pointing fingers and saying "well this and that and..." I take pride in everything I do, even when I mess up. Nobody is perfect, and that is okay. Part of life is to mess up and learn, so be accountable. 

Finally, respect. Treat others the way you want to be treated. If you want work done a specific way, lead by example. Do not 50% life becasue than you get 50% returns. You should always strive to get 110% back. These are my ethics and values of life and I carry them into any space I enter.
